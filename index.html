Boost performance with more RAM and storage

Introduction

The ASUS TUF Gaming F15 (FX507VV4-FX507VV) with an Intel Core i9-13900H and NVIDIA RTX 4060 is a powerful machine. Upgrading its RAM and storage can enhance multitasking, speed up load times, and future-proof your laptop. This guide walks you through the process with clear steps and visuals.

Disclaimer: Opening your laptop may void the warranty. Check with ASUS support before proceeding.

Tools Needed

  • Small Phillips screwdriver
  • Compatible RAM modules (e.g., DDR5, check ASUS specs)
  • M.2 NVMe SSD (if upgrading storage)
  • Anti-static wrist strap (optional, for safety)

Upgrading RAM

Step 1: Power Off and Unplug

Shut down your laptop completely and disconnect it from any power source to ensure safety.

Step 2: Remove the Bottom Panel

Use a Phillips screwdriver to remove the screws on the bottom panel. Gently lift it off, starting from a corner.

Step 3: Locate RAM Slots

Find the RAM slots, typically covered by a metal shield or easily accessible.

Step 4: Install New RAM

Remove existing modules (if replacing) by pushing the clips outward, then insert new RAM at a 45-degree angle and press down until it clicks.

Step 5: Reassemble

Replace the bottom panel, secure the screws, and power on the laptop to verify the upgrade.

Upgrading Storage

Step 1: Locate the M.2 Slot

After removing the bottom panel, find the M.2 slot, usually near the center or bottom of the motherboard.

Step 2: Remove Existing SSD (Optional)

If replacing an SSD, unscrew it and gently pull it out at an angle.

Step 3: Install New SSD

Insert the new SSD at a 45-degree angle, then press it down and secure it with the screw.

Step 4: Reassemble

Reattach the bottom panel, tighten the screws, and boot up to confirm the new storage is recognized.

Conclusion

You’ve successfully upgraded your ASUS TUF F15! Enjoy faster performance and more capacity. Double-check all connections and screws before regular use, and consider updating your BIOS or drivers for optimal compatibility.
